# 🌑 Dark Mods — Icarus

> A collection of mods designed to push **Icarus** further — harder environments, better gear, and expanded workshop options.

---

## 📦 Mods

---

### ⛈️ Dark-MoreHardStorms

> *Longer storms, higher tiers, fewer clear skies — ICARUS becomes truly unforgiving.*

Makes Icarus far more hostile by increasing storm frequency, duration, and intensity.
Weather phases last much longer, high-tier storms are common, and clear skies are rare.
Survival now depends on preparation, timing, and strong shelters.

### 🧥 Dark-UndersuitsPlus

> *Undersuits that actually feel worth wearing.*

Enhances all undersuits by improving their stats and overall usefulness, while keeping their original role and balance intact.
Designed to make undersuits feel more rewarding throughout progression, especially in harsher environments and longer expeditions.

**Features:**
- Improved undersuit stats
- Better survivability and efficiency
- Slots changed
- Fully compatible with vanilla progression
